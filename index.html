Understanding Autism

What is Autism?

Autism, or Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD), is a developmental condition that affects how people perceive the world and interact with others. It is characterized by challenges in social communication and repetitive behaviors.

Why Autism Awareness Matters

Raising awareness about autism helps foster understanding, acceptance, and inclusion for individuals on the spectrum. It encourages communities to support and celebrate neurodiversity.
